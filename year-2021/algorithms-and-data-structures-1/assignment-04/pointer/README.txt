Zeby uruchomić program, proszę wpisać:
make
./ex4Pointer

Operacje dominujące:

    push:
        stack.push_back->while;
        O(n);

    pop:
        stack.pop_back->for;
        O(n);
    
    peek:
        stack.pop_back->for;
        O(n);
    
    isEmpty:
        if;
        O(1);
    
    size:
        return;
        O(1);

    isFul:
        return;
        O(1);
